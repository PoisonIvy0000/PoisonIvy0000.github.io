Our Programs

PILATES FOR OLDER ADULTS

As people get older, they can lose some of their balance and coordination. Pilates increases strength and flexibility in both the core and the legs, which positively affects balance. This, along with basic fitness benefits, can help them reduce the risk of falls.

PRENATAL AND POSTNATAL PILATES

Strengthens your pelvic floor muscles. Postnatal Pilates can help to strengthen the pelvic floor muscles by teaching women how to reconnect and engage the pelvic floor muscles to improve core strength and stability.

PILATES FOR ATHLETES

Pilates gives athletes an edge mentally and physically. The practice reduces injuries, speeds recovering time, improves range of motion, promotes efficient breathing techniques, and develops a mind-body connection.

PILATES FOR OSTEOPOROSIS AND SCOLIOSIS

This program is designed to help you build your core, increase strength to support the bones, and improve balance to prevent falls. The benefits of Pilates done correctly for osteoporosis, will help you live a healthy life, while moving better and feeling better.

PILATES FOR WEIGHT LOSS

Pilates is a popular low-impact exercise. It's effective for toning up, building lean muscle, and improving posture. Practicing Pilates can be beneficial for your health and help you maintain a healthy weight.

Reformer Pilates

The reformer specifically targets your abs and obliques. It also strengthens your core which can help alleviate back pain, improve balance, reduce injury chances and tone stomach muscles. Reformer Pilates can be used as a sport specific cross training tool. It gives a full-body workout, which improves power, strength, flexibility and efficiency.

Chair Reformer Circuit

It activates the lower back, buttocks, shoulder, transverses abdominals and pelvic region and heightens neuromuscular coordination.It is excellent for strengthening the core muscles. However, because many exercises are upright it is also great for clients that are pregnant or those dealing with any type of vertigo.

Book Now

Core Suspend

With core suspend, exercises are designed to identify muscle imbalances and activate deep core muscles with inner joint stability to encourage proper biomechanics. With every joint in the proper position, segments of muscles learn proper movement patterns and activate with the correct amount at the correct time.

Book Now

Core Barre

Core Barre helps strengthen and tone your muscles without increasing bulk, and it improves your posture. It also increases cardiovascular endurance and metabolism, which helps to quickly burn calories. Regular barre workouts can increase your bone density, which can help prevent conditions like osteoporosis.

Book Now

Core HIIT

HIIT focuses on large, global muscles and Pilates works on the core, so when combined they work on both large and small muscle groups. This ensures a whole body focus. HIIT provides a quick and effective cardio workout, which is helpful for burning lots of calories, slowing down ageing and boosting the metabolism.

Book Now

Traditional Mat Pilates

Traditional Mat Pilates helps build a strong, balanced body and increases flexibility. As you progress in your practice you will notice improvements in breathing, focus, stress management, and body awareness. Mat Pilates is perfect for beginners because you only need your own body, a mat, and some floor space to start.
