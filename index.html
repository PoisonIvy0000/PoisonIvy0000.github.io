What you need to know?

Daily Body Aches and Pain?
Here's how Pilates can help:
  • Pilates improves core strength and function. The core is all the surrounding muscles of the trunk that when strengthened, support & stabilize the body.
  • Pilates focuses on developing dynamic strength, which means you are able to support and stabilize your joints while moving.
  • Rehabilitation and prevention of strains and injuries.
Pregnancy and Post-natal Pilates
Here's how Pilates can help:
  • Pilates, with its low impact emphasis on stabilizing the joints, can eliminate or manage many of the typical pregnancy-related aches and pains.
  • Pilates is a great way to reconnect with and strengthen your pelvic floor from giving birth.
  • Pilates restores confidence. It will teach you to listen, understand, & reconnect with your body as you exercise.
Pilates for Older Adults
Here's how Pilates can help:
  • Pilates helps improve the bone density of the elderly.
  • Pilates can improves stability & balance.
  • Pilates teaches them on how they can control their body. To prevent falls & injury.
  • Pilates has a breathing technique that strengthens the Respiratory muscles.
  • Proven to slow the effects of Parkinson's disease.
